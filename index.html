Safety Awareness Tips
Stay safe online — know the most common scam tactics used against people in India.
🚫
Never share OTP, PIN or Password. No bank, government or company will ever ask for these via SMS or email. Ever.
🔗
Don't click suspicious links. Scammers create fake websites identical to real ones. Always verify the URL before entering any details.
🏆
If you didn't enter, you didn't win. Lottery and prize scams are the most common. Ignore "you've won" messages from unknown numbers.
⏰
Urgency is always a red flag. "Act now!", "2 hours left!", "Account will be blocked" — scammers use fear and urgency to stop you from thinking.
